Roasted Strawberry Banana Bread

A Summer/Fall treat you will make every year

Roasted strawberry banana bread is delicious, moist, and the strawberry bring a beautiful sweetness to an already scrumptious dish.

Adding roasted strawberries to your banana bread takes a bit more time, but you will definitely save that down the road when you realize how quickly it disappears! Here's what you will need:

Amount Ingredient
1 1/4 Cups strawberries, diced
3 Ripe Medium Bananas, mashed
2 tbsp Canola Oil
1 1/4 Cups Whole Wheat Flour
3/4 tsp Baking Soda
1/4 tsp Salt
2 tbsp Butter, softened
1/2 cup Light Brown Sugar, not packed
2 Large Egg Whites
1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly spray a loaf pan with baking spray.
  2. Place the strawberries on a baking sheet and roast for 25 minutes, remove and set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt with a wire whisk. Set aside.
  4. In a large bowl cream butter and sugar with an electric mixer.
  5. Add egg whites, bananas, oil and vanilla, and beat at medium speed until thick. Scrape down sides of the bowl.
  6. Add flour mixture and blend at low speed until combined, do not over mix.
  7. Fold in strawberries and pour batter into the prepared loaf pans; bake in the center rack for about 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the pan cool at least 20 minutes, allow the bread to finish cooling on a wire rack, covered. It should be room temperature before slicing.

Helpful Tips

  • I use a silicon mat under the strawberries to help catch the extra juices for use in other things.
